4 Best refreshing beverages in Chandigarh
Himanshu Rathee
Last updated Apr 13, 2021
1.
Salted Caramel Chillatta at Cinnabon
SCO No. 302, Elante Mall, 3rd Floor, Industrial Area Phase I, Chandigarh, 160002

Salted Caramel Chillatta is a combination of freshly blended ice and sweet cream, swirled with salted caramel, and topped with whipped cream and caramel drizzle. Cinnabon's new limited-edition Salted Caramel Chillatta brings the salty dessert trend into the beverage world surprisingly well.

Recommendation: Salted Caramel Chillatta

2.
Masala Chai at Chaayos
178, MW Area, Industrial Area Phase I, Chandigarh, 160002

Masala chai is a tea beverage made by boiling black tea in milk and water with a mixture of aromatic herbs and spices. Originating in South Asia, the beverage has gained worldwide popularity, becoming a feature in many coffee and tea houses. Although traditionally prepared as a decoction of green cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, ground cloves, ground ginger, and black peppercorn together with black tea leaves, retail versions include tea bags for infusion, instant powdered mixtures, and concentrates.

Recommendation: Masala Chai

3.
Vanilla Latte at Starbucks
Elante Mall, Industrial Area Phase I, Chandigarh, 160002

Extra-smooth Starbucks® Blonde Espresso, velvety steamed milk and vanilla syrup come together to create a delightful new twist on a beloved espresso classic. An ideal cup for those who prefer a lighter-roasted coffee.The vanilla latte can be one of the best Starbucks drinks to order to keep health goals in check. And not to mention, all while satisfying the desire for a crafted coffee!

Recommendation: Vanilla Latte

4.
Coffee Cafe Mocha at Barista
Plot No, 27, Purv Marg, Industrial Area Phase I, Chandigarh, 160002

A caffè mocha, also called mocaccino, is a chocolate-flavoured variant of a caffè latte, commonly served in a glass. Other commonly used spellings are mochaccino and also mochachino. The name is derived from the city of Mocha, Yemen, which was one of the centers of early coffee trade. Like a caffè latte, caffè mocha is based on espresso and hot milk but with added chocolate flavouring and sweetener, typically in the form of cocoa powder and sugar. Many varieties use chocolate syrup instead, and some may contain dark or milk chocolate.

Recommendation: Coffee Cafe Mocha
